Delete All Your Personal Information

WARNING: This cannot be undone. There is no backup (if there were, we would need to delete your information in the backup also!).

What Is Deleted

This deletes all your personal information on the system, including:

  • All grades you have received, if you are a student.
  • All your profile information.
  • Your affiliation with all assignments.
  • All of your assignments, if you are a teacher, including all of the votes received by students in the assignments.

What Is NOT Deleted

  • Any files on Google Drive. Those files are under the control of the users, who can delete them if they so wish. The service has no longer access to them.
  • The log files. Those are deleted automatically after some time, as per privacy policy.

Are You Sure?

If you delete it, it's all gone -- POOF! If you are a student, make sure you don't need any grades. If you are a teacher, make sure you have downloaded any grades you need.
