Obtain New Notebook

Assignment: [[=assignment_name]]

If your notebook becomes corrupted, or if you cannot access it (because, e.g., you inadvertently deleted it), you can obtain a new copy.

If you do so, the existing notebook (if any) is not deleted, so that you can copy your work to the new notebook. If you obtain a new copy of the notebook, it is the new copy that will be graded.
